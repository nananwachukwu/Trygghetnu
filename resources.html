Social Services (Quick Help)

Social Services (Socialtjänsten) in your municipality can provide emergency housing, financial aid, and support.

  • During office hours: Contact your local municipality office.
  • After hours: Call 112 and ask for the "Social services on call" (Socialjouren).

If You Need to Leave Home Immediately

Try to remember (if you can):

  • ID documents for you and children
  • Bank cards, proof of accounts
  • House keys
  • Important phone numbers (memorize or write down)
  • Essential items for children (stuffed animals, clothes)
  • Prescriptions, medicines

Even without these - LEAVE. Your safety comes first.

Types of Violence

Violence isn't just physical. It can also be:

  • Psychological: Threats, isolation, control, verbal abuse.
  • Sexual: Forcing sexual acts, harassment.
  • Material: Destroying property, hurting pets.
  • Financial: Controlling money, not allowing you to work.
  • Latent: Constant fear, threatening body language.
  • Neglect: Withholding food, medicine, or care.

Information based on official Swedish sources (Försäkringskassan, Migration Agency, Police).
