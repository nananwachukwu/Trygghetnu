Get Help Now - Escape Safely

You have the right to live free from violence. If you need to leave immediately, call 112.

1. I Need Help NOW

If you are in immediate danger, call the emergency services.

CALL 112 (Emergency)

For anonymous support and advice (24/7):

116 016 (Kvinnofridslinjen)

Calls to 116 016 are free and do not show up on your phone bill in Sweden.

2. I Have Nothing - No Money, No ID

You can still get help. Social services are required to help you with emergency housing and food even if you have no identification or money.

View Resources

3. Help a Friend

If you are concerned about someone else, you can use our templates to reach out safely or find professional support lines.
