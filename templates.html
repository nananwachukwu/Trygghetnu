Email & SMS Templates

Copy these templates to use on public computers or borrowed phones.

1. Asking a Friend for Help (Email)

Subject: I need your help - urgent but I'm safe Dear [Friend's Name], I'm writing to you because I trust you. I've had to leave my situation suddenly and I'm safe, but I left with nothing - no phone, no money, no ID. I'm at a library using a public computer. I need your help to get back on my feet. If you're willing to help: 1. Reply to this email and ask me a question only the real me would know (to verify it's really me). 2. Once I answer correctly, we can arrange help. 3. The safest way to send money without ID is Western Union/MoneyGram to a name I'll give you, with a test question, or e-gift cards (ICA, Coop, H&M) sent by email. I understand if you need time to verify this is really me. Please ask me something private. Thank you for being someone I could trust.

2. Contacting Social Services (Email)

Subject: Help needed - fleeing domestic violence To Social Services in [Municipality Name], I am writing to request emergency assistance. I have fled a domestic violence situation and have no money, no phone, and no identification with me. I am currently at [location - e.g., a library in central Stockholm]. I need: - Emergency housing/shelter - Financial assistance for basic needs - Help replacing my ID documents I can be reached at this email address temporarily. Please respond with instructions on what to do next. I am safe right now but need urgent support. Thank you, [Your Name or a temporary name]

3. SMS Template

"FriendName, it's [YourName]. I've had to escape and have nothing - no money, no phone. I'm safe but need help. I'm at a library using a borrowed computer. Can you email me at [temporary email]? I'll explain. Please ask me something only I would know to verify. This is not a joke."

How to Send Money Without ID

If a friend wants to help but doesn't know how:

  • Western Union / MoneyGram: They can send cash to a name you provide. Set a "test question" (e.g., "Favorite pet?"). You can pick up cash with just the name and correct answer at many grocery stores or Pressbyrån.
  • E-gift cards: They can buy digital gift cards (ICA, Coop, Pressbyrån) and email the codes to you. You can use these immediately for food or phone cards.

Always double-check the recipient's email address before sending sensitive information.
